# Quick Start Guide - New Features 🚀

## Version 1.1.0 - Phase 1 & 2 Features

### 🎯 For Students

#### 1. **5-Minute Challenges** ⚡
**Access:** Click "⚡ 5-Min Challenges" on home page or visit `/micro-learning`

**What it is:**
- Quick 5-minute learning sessions
- Perfect for before school or bedtime
- Complete 3 per day to reach your goal

**How to use:**
1. Choose a challenge (Morning Boost, Maths Challenge, etc.)
2. Complete 5 questions in 5 minutes
3. Earn points and track progress

---

#### 2. **Daily Missions** 📋
**Access:** Visible on home dashboard

**What it is:**
- 5 new missions every day
- Earn bonus points, coins, and badges
- Resets at midnight

**Mission Types:**
- Complete 3 quizzes
- Answer 20 questions correctly
- Score 80%+ in a subject
- Maintain your learning streak
- Spend 30 minutes learning

---

#### 3. **Virtual Pet** 🐉
**Access:** Pet widget on home page

**What it is:**
- Your own digital pet that grows with you
- Feed it, play with it, level it up
- Evolves as you learn more

**How to care:**
- Feed when hungry (🍎 Feed button)
- Play when bored (🎾 Play button)
- Complete quizzes to level up
- Watch it evolve from egg → baby → teen → adult

**Pet Types:**
- Dragon 🐉
- Unicorn 🦄
- Phoenix 🔥🦅
- Owl 🦉
- Fox 🦊

---

#### 4. **Voice Commands** 🎤
**Access:** Purple floating button (bottom right)

**What it is:**
- Control the app with your voice
- Have questions read aloud
- Get voice feedback

**Available Commands:**
- "Go home" - Back to homepage
- "Start quiz" - Begin a quiz
- "Leaderboard" - View rankings
- "My progress" - See your stats
- "Battle" - Start quiz battle
- "Help" - Hear all commands

**How to use:**
1. Click the microphone button
2. Wait for "Listening..."
3. Say your command clearly
4. Listen for confirmation

---

#### 5. **Offline Learning** 📡
**Access:** Automatic - no setup needed

**What it is:**
- Complete quizzes even without internet
- Your progress saves automatically
- Syncs when connection returns

**How it works:**
1. Go offline (no WiFi/data)
2. Complete quizzes as normal
3. See "Offline Mode" indicator
4. Go back online
5. Watch quizzes sync automatically

**Indicators:**
- 🟠 Offline Mode - No connection
- 🔵 Syncing... - Uploading progress
- 🟢 Synced! - All saved

---

#### 6. **Smart Help** 💡
**Access:** Automatic when struggling

**What it is:**
- App detects when you need help
- Offers easier questions
- Suggests video tutorials
- Alerts parents if needed

**What happens:**
- Fail 3 times → "Try easier questions?"
- Fail 4 times → "Watch a video tutorial?"
- Fail 5 times → Parent gets notified

**Don't worry:** This is to help, not punish! Everyone struggles sometimes.

### 🎮 Quick Tips

#### Maximize Your Points
1. Complete daily missions (+50-100 points each)
2. Maintain your streak (bonus multiplier)
3. Use streak protection wisely (2 available)
4. Try 5-minute challenges (quick points)
5. Level up your pet (motivation boost)

#### Best Times to Learn
- **Morning (7-9am):** "Morning Brain Boost" challenge
- **After School (4-6pm):** Daily missions
- **Before Bed (7-9pm):** "Bedtime Brain Teaser"

#### Struggling with a Topic?
1. Watch the intervention video
2. Try easier difficulty
3. Take a break and come back
4. Ask parent/teacher for help
5. Use "Review Mode" to practice

### 🐛 Troubleshooting

#### Voice Commands Not Working
- ✓ Check microphone permission
- ✓ Use Chrome/Safari (not Firefox)
- ✓ Speak clearly after "Listening..."
- ✓ Try saying "help" first

#### Offline Sync Failed
- ✓ Check internet connection
- ✓ Refresh page
- ✓ Wait 30 seconds and retry
- ✓ Check localStorage not full

#### Pet Not Responding
- ✓ Pet might be sleeping (low energy)
- ✓ Feed if hungry (hunger > 80)
- ✓ Refresh page
- ✓ Check you tapped the pet widget

#### Missions Not Updating
- ✓ Complete the quiz fully
- ✓ Refresh the missions panel
- ✓ Check it's the same day (resets at midnight)
- ✓ Make sure you're logged in
